About Us

AIHIKA Constructions is a professionally driven civil engineering and construction company engaged in the execution of residential, commercial and industrial projects. Established with a strong foundation of technical expertise and practical site experience, the company is committed to delivering high-quality construction solutions that meet the evolving demands of modern infrastructure development.

The organization is led by qualified civil engineering professionals with exposure to reputed builders and multinational Project Management Consultancy (PMC) environments. This experience enables AIHIKA Constructions to operate with a structured and systematic approach, ensuring strict compliance with approved drawings, technical specifications, IS codes, contractual requirements and statutory safety regulations.

AIHIKA Constructions places a strong emphasis on disciplined execution, effective coordination and transparent communication with clients, consultants and stakeholders. Each project is executed with a focus on quality assurance, safety management, cost control and timely delivery, ensuring that client expectations are consistently met or exceeded.

With a growing portfolio of residential, commercial and industrial projects, the company continues to strengthen its capabilities in RCC works, structural execution, turnkey project delivery and site management. The team adopts best construction practices, modern methodologies and efficient resource planning to ensure smooth project execution across all stages.

At AIHIKA Constructions, we believe that trust, quality and consistency are the key pillars of long-term success. Our commitment is not just to construct buildings, but to deliver reliable, durable and value-driven infrastructure solutions that stand the test of time.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a trusted and reliable construction partner delivering high-quality infrastructure with excellence, integrity and professionalism, while contributing to sustainable development and long-term client relationships.

Our Mission

To execute construction projects with uncompromised quality, safety and efficiency by adopting systematic project management practices, maintaining transparency in all operations and continuously improving through innovation, technical expertise and commitment to excellence.

Health, Safety & Environment (HSE) Policy

AIHIKA Constructions is committed to maintaining the highest standards of health, safety and environmental protection across all its projects. The company ensures that all construction activities are carried out in compliance with applicable safety regulations, statutory requirements and industry best practices.

We adopt a proactive approach towards hazard identification, risk assessment and implementation of preventive measures to ensure a safe working environment for all personnel. Regular safety briefings, training programs and site inspections are conducted to promote safety awareness and minimize risks.

Our objective is to achieve zero accidents, ensure the well-being of all stakeholders and execute projects responsibly while minimizing environmental impact.

Quality Policy

AIHIKA Constructions is dedicated to delivering construction projects that meet the highest standards of quality and performance. The company ensures strict adherence to approved drawings, technical specifications, relevant IS codes and contractual requirements throughout the project lifecycle.

Quality is maintained through systematic planning, proper material selection, continuous monitoring of site activities and implementation of quality control procedures at every stage of construction.

Our commitment is to achieve excellence in workmanship, ensure client satisfaction and continuously improve our processes to deliver reliable and durable infrastructure solutions.

Experience Summary

The leadership team of AIHIKA Constructions brings over a decade of combined experience in civil engineering and construction, with exposure to residential, commercial and industrial projects.

The team has worked closely with reputed builders and multinational Project Management Consultancy (PMC) firms, gaining valuable experience in structured project execution, quality management systems, safety compliance and coordination with multiple stakeholders.

This experience enables AIHIKA Constructions to understand and deliver projects in line with corporate and PMC standards, ensuring professional execution, effective planning and timely completion of works.
