About Us

AIHIKA Constructions is a professionally managed civil engineering company specializing in residential, commercial and industrial projects. With strong technical expertise and exposure to reputed builders and PMC environments, we ensure structured execution and timely delivery. We strictly follow drawings, specifications and quality standards. Our focus is on delivering durable and value-driven infrastructure. Client satisfaction remains our top priority.

Vision

To be a trusted construction partner delivering high-quality infrastructure with integrity and professionalism. We aim to build long-term relationships through consistent performance. Our vision is to contribute to modern and sustainable development.

Mission

To execute projects with uncompromised quality, safety and efficiency. We follow systematic planning and transparent communication. Our goal is timely completion and client satisfaction. Continuous improvement is key to our growth.

HSE Policy

We maintain high standards of health, safety and environmental protection. Safety practices, training and inspections are implemented across all projects. Our objective is zero accidents and safe execution. Environmental impact is minimized during construction.

Quality Policy

We ensure strict adherence to IS codes, specifications and approved drawings. Quality checks are done at every stage. Proper materials and methods are used. Our commitment is to deliver reliable and high-quality structures.

Experience Summary

Our leadership team brings over a decade of combined experience in civil construction. We have worked with reputed builders and PMC firms. Expertise includes RCC works and turnkey execution. This enables professional and efficient project delivery.
