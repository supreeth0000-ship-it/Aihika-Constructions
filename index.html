About Us

AIHIKA Constructions is a professionally managed civil engineering and construction company engaged in the execution of residential, commercial and infrastructure projects. The organization is driven by strong technical expertise, disciplined site execution practices and a clear focus on quality, safety and timely completion of works.

The company operates with strict adherence to contractual requirements, approved drawings, technical specifications, quality assurance plans and statutory safety norms. AIHIKA Constructions follows structured project management systems, proactive coordination with clients, consultants and statutory authorities, and robust site control mechanisms to ensure smooth project delivery.

With leadership having strong exposure to PMC-driven and corporate projects, the company is well-positioned to execute projects that demand high levels of compliance, coordination and execution discipline.
