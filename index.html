About Us

AIHIKA Constructions, established in 2018, is a professionally managed civil engineering and construction company engaged in the execution of residential, commercial and industrial projects. With over 7+ years of industry experience, the organization is built on a strong foundation of technical expertise, practical site exposure and structured project execution methodologies.

With exposure to reputed builders and multinational Project Management Consultancy (PMC) environments, we bring a systematic and disciplined approach to every project undertaken. We operate with strict adherence to approved drawings, technical specifications, relevant IS codes and contractual requirements.

Our execution strategy focuses on proper planning, resource optimization and coordination with clients, consultants and vendors. This ensures smooth workflow, minimized delays and effective cost control across all stages of construction. We emphasize quality assurance, safety compliance and timely project delivery in every project.

Vision

Our vision is to be recognized as a trusted and reliable construction partner delivering high-quality infrastructure with integrity, professionalism and consistency. We aim to build long-term client relationships by maintaining superior execution standards and exceeding expectations in every project.

We strive to contribute to sustainable and modern infrastructure development by adopting innovative construction techniques and efficient project management practices. Our focus is on creating value-driven and durable structures.

Mission

Our mission is to execute construction projects with uncompromised quality, safety and efficiency by adopting systematic planning and disciplined execution practices. We ensure timely completion while maintaining strict control over quality and cost.

We are committed to continuous improvement through innovation, technical expertise and transparent communication, ensuring complete client satisfaction and long-term professional relationships.

Health, Safety & Environment (HSE) Policy

AIHIKA Constructions is committed to maintaining the highest standards of health, safety and environmental protection across all projects. We strictly follow statutory regulations, safety procedures and site-specific safety plans to ensure a secure working environment.

We implement proactive measures such as hazard identification, risk assessment and preventive controls. Regular safety training, toolbox talks and inspections are conducted to ensure compliance and awareness.

Our objective is to achieve zero accidents and ensure responsible construction practices while minimizing environmental impact.

Quality Policy

We are dedicated to delivering construction projects that meet the highest standards of quality and performance. Strict adherence to approved drawings, IS codes and technical specifications is maintained at all stages.

Quality control is ensured through systematic inspection, monitoring and proper execution methods. We focus on skilled workmanship and continuous supervision to achieve consistent results.

Our commitment is to deliver reliable, durable and high-quality infrastructure that meets client expectations.

Experience Summary

With over 9+ years of experience since our establishment in 2017, the leadership team has gained strong exposure to residential, commercial and industrial projects. Our experience includes working with reputed builders and multinational PMC firms.

Our expertise covers RCC works, structural execution, finishing works and turnkey project delivery. We have developed strong capabilities in planning, execution management, quality assurance and safety compliance.

This experience enables us to execute projects professionally with efficiency, precision and timely delivery, aligning with corporate and industry standards.
